Feng Office Help

Feng Office is a complex application developed with the effort of many contributors from the open source community. A manual is constantly being developed by volunteers. When the Feng Office manual is ready it will be available at Feng Office's site at fengoffice.com/web/wiki.

You can get help and and answers to your questions at the Feng Office forums: fengoffice.com/web/forums.

If you want to help Feng Office become the best WebOffice contact us at contact@fengoffice.com or through the forums. You can help by reporting bugs, giving us your suggestions and feedback or contributing with your knowledge in either programming, documentation writing, graphic design, marketing, etc.

Thank you for using Feng Office!
